LPC Character Portraits — Credits
=================================

The portraits in this folder were composed from the Liberated Pixel Cup (LPC)
Universal Spritesheet asset library. They are NOT official MapleStory assets.

Composition notes
-----------------
22 job idle portraits (down-facing) were layered offline (body + clothes/armor +
hair + feet + weapon) and recolored for job identity. Scale: 64×64 → 256×256
nearest-neighbor.

Also used in this project (separate CC0 packs):
  - Kenney audio (CC0) — public/audio/
  - OpenGameArt Town Theme RPG / Battle Theme A (CC0) by cynicmusic
  - 496 RPG icons (CC0) — public/icons/

In-game battlefield unit animation still uses original procedural pixel sprites
from this project (not LPC / not MapleStory official art).
